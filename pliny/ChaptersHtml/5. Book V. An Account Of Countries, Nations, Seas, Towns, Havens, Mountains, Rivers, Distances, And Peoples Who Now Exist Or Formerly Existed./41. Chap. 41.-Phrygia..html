On its northern side it borders upon Galatia, on the south it joins Lyeaonia, Pisidia, and Mygdonia, and, on the east, it touches upon Cappadocia. The more celebrated towns there, besides those already mentioned, are Ancyra[2], Andria, Cel¾n¾[3], Coloss¾[4], Carina[5], Cotyaion[6], Ceraine, Conium, and Midaium.
