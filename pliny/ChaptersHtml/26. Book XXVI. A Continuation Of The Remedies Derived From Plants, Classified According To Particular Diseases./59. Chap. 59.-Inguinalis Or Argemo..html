CHAP. 59.ÑINGUINALIS OR ARGEMO.

Inguinalis[1] again, or, as some persons call it, "argemo," a plant commonly found growing in bushes and thickets, needs only to be held in the hand to be productive of beneficial effects upon the groin.

1. The "Groin plant." Probably the came as the Bubonion of B. xxvii. c. 19.
