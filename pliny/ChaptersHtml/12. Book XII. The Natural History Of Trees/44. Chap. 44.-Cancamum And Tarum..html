CHAP. 44.—CANCAMUM AND TARUM.

From the confines of the country which produces cinnamon and cassia, cancamum[1] and tarum[2] are imported; but these substances are brought by way of the Nabatæan Troglodytæ, a colony of the Nabatæi.

1. A gum resin of some unknown species, but not improbably, Fée thinks, the produce of some of the Amyrides. Sprengel thinks that it was produced from the Gardenia gummifera.

2. Aloe-wood.
