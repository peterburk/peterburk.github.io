CHAP. 42.ÑTHE SILLYBUM.

The sillybum[1] resembles the white cham¾leon, and is a plant quite as prickly. In Cilicia, Syria, and Phoenicia, the countries where it grows, it is not thought worth while to boil it, the cooking of it being so extremely troublesome, it is said. It is of no use whatever in medicine.
